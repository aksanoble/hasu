How Hasu Works

Hasu is a personal todo app that runs entirely on your Supabase project. Supakey handles OAuth and one‑click schema deployment so your data stays private and under your control.

Architecture: Hasu ↔ Supakey ↔ Your Supabase

1) Sign in with Supakey

You authenticate with Supakey using OAuth. No service keys are ever exposed in the browser.

2) One‑click schema deploy

Supakey deploys Hasu’s schema into your Supabase (a dedicated schema per app) and applies RLS + grants.

3) App‑scoped tokens

Supakey mints app‑specific user tokens (JWT + refresh). Hasu connects directly to your Supabase using those tokens.
